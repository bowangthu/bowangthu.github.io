About Me

Now, I’m an assistant professor at Tsinghua University. Before that, I was a Postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University working with Prof. Jianping Wu during July 2019 - October 2021. In June 2019, I received my Ph.D. in computer science and technology from the Tsinghua University under the supervision of Prof. Fengyuan Ren. I received my B.S. from the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2014.

Research Interests

Real-Time Communications (RTC), Live streaming, VoD streaming
Bandwidth measurement and prediction
